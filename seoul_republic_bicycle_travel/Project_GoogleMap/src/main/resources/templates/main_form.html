About Seoul

Seoul has been the capital of Korea for more than 600 years since the Joseon Dynasty (1392-1910). It was referred to as "Hanyang"during the Joseon Dynasty.Today's Seoul is a bustling metropolis, acting as the hub for political, economical, socialand cultural matters. The Hangang River, which runs through the center of the city, divides Seoul into the culture and history-oriented northern region, and the business-oriented southern region.
